About Sindkhed Raja


Sindkhed Raja is a town and a municipal council in Buldhana district in the Indian state of Maharashtra.

  • It is the birthplace of Rajmata Jijabai, mother of Shivaji.

  • Elevation : 549 m

  • Population : 16,434 (2011)

  • Language : Marathi

  • Villages : 108
